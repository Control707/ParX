Keep your spot

We want people to reserve a parking spot for special events so they wont have to spend time looking for one and missing out.

Here's how it works

Pick a Spot

You first choose a parking lot with available space near your event. You schedule the time you want it to be reserved and the information that it will be saved under. Once paid, the spot is yours!

Arrive

You arrive to the parking spot that you have reserved and your vehicle will be allowed to stay there for as long as the reservation is in effect, and you can enjoy your event knowing that you'll be there on time.

Protected

You can now ensure that you won't have to worry about having your car stay for too long and being towed, or being distracted from the event to extend a timer. The price when you reserve is all that you will need to pay!

Park confidently

Leave your car knowing that it's staying there for good.

Using ParX lets you be where you need to be while making sure that you're not stressed about recieving a citation.
